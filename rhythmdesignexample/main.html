About the Event

The Annual youth Festival-Rhythm is organized with the objective of developing admiration for rich Indian culture among student of science and Technology. With a large variety of cultural events, competitions, performance and contest, the campus is transform into a rich diversity showing a different type of Indian cultures....

Colleges from all over the country flocks over while competing and displaying vary skills, cultures and genres. Rhythm provides students opportunities to develop the traits of mutual understand, cooperation, brotherhood and patriotism as well as an outlet for their creative activities.
